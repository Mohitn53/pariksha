Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about PARIKSHA and how it can help you succeed.

How do I get started with PARIKSHA?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, explore the available practice tests, and start practicing. You can also create custom tests or generate specialized ones based on your learning needs.

Are the practice tests free to use?

Yes, we offer a range of free practice tests for all users. Premium subscribers get access to additional advanced tests, detailed analytics, and personalized recommendations.

Can I create my own custom tests?

Absolutely! Our "Create Test" feature allows you to design custom tests with your own questions, options, and difficulty levels. You can share these tests with others or keep them private for personal practice.

Can I track my progress over time?

Yes, PARIKSHA provides detailed analytics to help you track your performance. View your test history, analyze your strengths and weaknesses, and monitor your improvement over time through your personal dashboard.
